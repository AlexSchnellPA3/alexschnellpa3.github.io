Issues That Give People Influence

Pocketbook issues are not partisan. They are structural issues that determine whether people have a real say in how they are governed.

Influence belongs to the people, not political machines.
Trust requires transparency and accountability.

Term Limits

Career politicians distort representation. I support limiting House members to three terms and Senators to two. Even if Congress refuses, I commit to no more than three terms myself.

Transparency and Accountability

Trust requires transparency. That means audits, protected whistleblowers, and releasing long-withheld government files including those related to Epstein, MLK, JFK, and others.

Clean Money in Politics

We do not represent people in other states. Campaign donations should come from within the district. Corporate lobbying and outside money dilute local voices.

Ballot Access and Independent Voters

Independent voters face unfair barriers in Pennsylvania. Short signature windows and high thresholds protect incumbents, not voters. Representation should not require party loyalty.
