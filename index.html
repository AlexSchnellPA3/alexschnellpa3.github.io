The Person Behind @AlexSchnellPA3 on X

Hello, everyone. I am Alex Schnell, a member of the Philadelphia 9th Ward Republican Committee, and I am exploring a grassroots run for Congress in Pennsylvania’s 3rd District. My interest in public service grew from years of community involvement and from a professional background focused on analysis, problem solving, and practical results.

My interest in technology began early in life through projects I worked on with my father. We built weather stations, created videos with green screen effects, and experimented with robotics and circuitry. These experiences encouraged me to pursue technical work and shaped the way I approach complex problems.

In high school, I assisted with the IT Lab and Lab Manager programs and earned two Microsoft certifications after graduation. During college, I focused on gaining practical experience. I earned a SAS Certificate in Business Intelligence and Analytics and a Leadership Certificate from the Oskin Leadership Institute. I completed internships, including one in the office of my Congressman, and I served as President of Widener’s foreign language honor society and Treasurer of the Catholic club.

After college, I spent four years at Breakaway Technologies. When that position ended, I used the skills I had gained to build a custom database for stock market forecasting. I also became more involved in public service and was elected as a local Committeeperson. This role strengthened my interest in government and community engagement and helped shape my decision to explore a run for Congress.

I currently work as a Business Analyst through a partnership between my company and the Philadelphia Parking Authority. My responsibilities involve improving processes, supporting operations, and helping the organization function efficiently. These experiences have reinforced my belief that effective representation requires clear communication, careful analysis, and consistent engagement with the people you serve.

I consider myself a Constitutionalist. I value limited government, individual liberty, and accountability. I do not align fully with either major party. I am choosing the path that allows a community-focused campaign to function effectively and reach the people who live here. My goal is to build a campaign that reflects the district and encourages residents to take part in shaping their representation.

This is a grassroots campaign.
This website is hosted on GitHub. Anyone can propose changes. Nothing is added without review. Transparency is built into the structure.

Why I Am Running

Many residents feel that their concerns do not reach Washington. They want a representative who spends time in the district, listens to local voices, and understands the issues that affect daily life. I believe effective representation begins with consistent engagement in the community.

My goal is to build a campaign that grows from the ground up. Community first. Local involvement. Clear communication. These principles guide my approach and shape the work I hope to do on behalf of the district.
