The Person Behind @AlexSchnellPA3

Hello, everyone. I’m Alex Schnell, a committee person for Philadelphia’s 9th Ward, and I’m exploring a run for Congress to represent Pennsylvania’s 3rd District.

I’m tired of seeing our communities divided by political labels that don’t reflect how people actually live. Everywhere I go, people care about the same things: safe neighborhoods, honest government, strong schools, and leaders who show up.

I went to rallies across the political spectrum not to pick sides, but to listen. I spoke with Democrats, Republicans, and independents while canvassing and in daily life. What I found was unity being drowned out by a system that benefits from division.

This is a grassroots campaign.
This website is hosted on GitHub. Anyone can propose changes. Nothing is added without review. Transparency is built into the structure.

Why I’m Running

Too often, representatives govern from Washington instead of representing their districts. They sit far away, make rules for the entire country, and rarely return to the communities they’re supposed to serve.

I believe representation should be bottom-up. Community first. Local voices matter. I focus on what I call pocketbook issues because they give people real influence over their government, regardless of party.
