Bottom-Up Representation

This campaign is built on direct involvement from the people who live in the district. I believe representation should grow from the community and reflect the priorities of the residents who experience the results of federal decisions every day.

The website is hosted on GitHub to support open participation. Anyone can propose improvements or corrections. Every suggestion is reviewed before it is added. This structure encourages transparency and invites residents to take part in the work of building a stronger district.

My Approach to Party Alignment

I consider myself a Constitutionalist. My views do not fit neatly within either major party. To run a community-focused campaign, I need a practical path that allows me to meet the legal requirements for ballot access and reach voters across the district. The Republican ballot line provides that path in this election cycle. My commitment remains centered on the people of the district and on the principles that guide my work.

How You Can Help

A grassroots campaign depends on steady involvement from residents. Every conversation and every connection strengthens the effort to build a more responsive form of representation.

  • Attend community meetings
  • Talk with neighbors about local concerns
  • Share ideas or suggestions through the contact page
  • Volunteer with local organizations or civic groups
  • Get creative!
